## Attribution Requirements

Under the SIL Open Font License, you must include attribution when distributing
the fonts with your software. You can satisfy this requirement by:

1. Including this NOTICE.md file in your distribution
2. Including the individual LICENSE.txt files
3. Displaying credits in your application's About screen using `AccessibleFonts.allAttributions`
